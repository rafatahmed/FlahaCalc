FAO Penman-Monteith Methodology

The FAO Penman-Monteith equation is the international standard for calculating reference evapotranspiration (ETo). It combines energy balance with mass transfer methods to provide accurate estimates of crop water requirements.

Our calculator implements the complete methodology as described in FAO Irrigation and Drainage Paper No. 56, accounting for all relevant climate variables:

  • Temperature
  • Wind Speed
  • Humidity
  • Solar Radiation
  • Elevation
  • Geographic Location
