Welcome to Flaha ETo Calculator

This web application calculates reference evapotranspiration (ETo) using the FAO Penman-Monteith method, which is the standard for estimating crop water requirements in agricultural and irrigation planning.

About Evapotranspiration

Evapotranspiration (ET) is the combined process of water surface evaporation, soil moisture evaporation, and plant transpiration. Reference ET (ETo) represents the evapotranspiration rate from a reference surface, not short of water.

The FAO Penman-Monteith method is widely recognized as the most accurate method for calculating ETo across different regions and climates.
