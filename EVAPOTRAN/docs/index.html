What is Evapotranspiration?

Evapotranspiration (ET) is the combined process of water evaporation from soil and plant surfaces and transpiration from plants. Reference evapotranspiration (ET₀) represents the evapotranspiration rate from a reference surface, not short of water. Understanding ET₀ is crucial for irrigation management and water resource planning.
