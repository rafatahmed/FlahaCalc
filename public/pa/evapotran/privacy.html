Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2023

Introduction

At Flaha Agri Tech, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you about how we look after your personal data when you visit our website and tell you about your privacy rights and how the law protects you.

Data We Collect

When you use EVAPOTRAN, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Usage data through Google Analytics, including pages visited, time spent on pages, and browser information
  • Location data if you use our live weather feature (city level only)
  • Account information if you create an account (email and password)
  • Weather data that you upload or generate

How We Use Your Data

We use your data for the following purposes:

  • To provide and improve our services
  • To understand how users interact with our application
  • To remember your preferences and settings
  • To secure your account and data

Analytics

We use Google Analytics to collect anonymous usage data. This helps us understand how visitors use our application so we can improve it. The following information may be collected:

  • Pages visited
  • Time spent on pages
  • Browser and device information
  • Approximate geographic location (country level)

No personally identifiable information is collected through Google Analytics. All data is processed in accordance with Google's privacy policy.

Cookies

Google Analytics uses cookies to identify unique visitors and sessions. These cookies do not contain any personal information.

Opting Out

You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by:

  • Using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on
  • Enabling "Do Not Track" in your browser
  • Disabling JavaScript in your browser

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or our data practices, please contact us at privacy@flaha.org.
